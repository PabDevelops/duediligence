## FILMING TIPS

**Phone Setup:**
- Good lighting (natural light from window, or ring light)
- Stabilize phone (tripod or prop it up)
- Quiet room (that's why you need good mic)
- Portrait mode (vertical video for TikTok)

**Recording:**
- Do 2-3 takes per script
- Read naturally (not robotic)
- Pause at key moments (let visuals breathe)
- Start with hook STRONG (first 3 seconds make or break)

**B-roll (screen recordings):**
- On your phone, open Traqcker
- Record yourself clicking, voting, navigating
- Zoom in on key elements (Easy Mode Score ring, achievement popup, etc)

**Editing:**
- Use CapCut (free, TikTok-native)
- Add text overlays at key moments
- Match cuts to music beats
- Add trending sounds (check TikTok Discover what's trending)
