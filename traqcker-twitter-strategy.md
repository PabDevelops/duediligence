## TONE GUIDELINES

✅ DO:
- Be authentic (mention the frustration that started this)
- Show the vision (why this matters beyond just a tool)
- Celebrate community (voting, achievements, user feedback)
- Use specific examples (Stock of the Week, Easy Mode Score, achievements)
- Professional but conversational (no corporate jargon)

❌ DON'T:
- Compare to competitors directly
- Make financial advice claims
- Over-hype (let the product speak)
- Use too many emojis (max 1 per tweet)
- Make promises about future features
